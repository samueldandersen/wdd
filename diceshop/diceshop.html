Welcome to Elemental Rolls online shop! Based in the wonderful city of Rexburg in the state of Idaho, it is here that you can fulfill your deepest desires by getting the dice of your dreams, and for a cheap price at that. Whether you are playing a game of Dungeons and Dragons Fifth Edition, or maybe playing once of the many iterations of Shadowrun, or a game of Call of Cthulhu, If its its dice you want, its dice you get, and of your own creation. Here is a place where your dice are one hundred percent customizable, from material to size, to font, its all fair game. Go crazy with it player!
