Overview

Purpose

The Website is a online Dice shop for Tabletop Roleplaying Games enthusiast. I am a huge fans of games like Dungeons and Dragons and Shadowrun, most of which use a variety of dice in order to play. Because dice are such a simple object, they are also extremely customizable, so people get them in all sorts of materials and colors. but sometimes, you want something very specific, and cant just find them anywhere. That's where the Website comes in. It would be a place to make custom dice, to add shapes and colors to the designs of them. While there are crafters out there, and places where you can upload your own design, my website is place for the layman to make, and preview design in a user friendly medium. The company of Hero Forge does a similar idea, except for miniatures. Hero Forge is very popular, so can we!

Audience

The audience for my diceshop is people who enjoy playing table top roleplaying games. I dont feel the strong need to state a age range, due to to the fact that these types of games are played by people of all ages, but maybe we could pander to people who care about how things look.

Home page

Welcome to Elemental Rolls online shop! Based in the wonderful city of Rexburg in the state of Idaho, it is here that you can fulfill your deepest desires by getting the dice of your dreams, and for a cheap price at that. Whether you are playing a game of Dungeons and Dragons Fifth Edition, or maybe playing once of the many iterations of Shadowrun, or a game of Call of Cthulhu, If its its dice you want, its dice you get, and of your own creation. Here is a place where your dice are one hundred percent customizable, from material to size, to font, its all fair game. Go crazy with it player! Here are our Flowerful Quartz dice. Made from a strong and sturdy resin, these beautiful dice have tiny flowers floating within them, as well as shiny pieces of white quartz crystal to truly dazzle your party, and your foes. Up above are the Midnight Forrest dice, carved from a mahogany wood, these dice are sure to invoke images of staying around a campfire that keeps the surrounding darkness at bay. Beautifully balanced. Here is the display of the Shining Sea dice, Made from turquoise crystal, you can easily imagine this set of dice rolling across your table in the same way that wave roll across the sands of the beach

Design Your Own Dice

Welcome to the "Design your own Dice' Page. First step is first, you get to choose the size of your dice. we have 4 sizes, from Large, Medium, Small, and Extra Small. Next, you get to choose the material that your custom set is made out of. Elemental roll grants a spread of options, including Resin, Wood, Metal, Gem, and Plastic. Next you get to the font that the lettering comes in. we grant total of 6 options, Arial, Calibri, Cambria, Comic Sans, Constantia, and Corbel. Now that you have chosen the Size, Material, and Font for your set of custom dice, it is now time to select the base color. Be warned, depending on the material that you chose, you may be more limited in your color options and hues available. Now that you have chosen the base color of your custom dice set, you may now choose some Flair. Again, these options may be restricted, or expanded depending on what material you chose. The options include Stickers, Floating Objects, and Additional Colors. Congratulations, you have now completed your custom set of Elemental Roll dice. All that is now left to do is for you to click on the check out button to complete your purchase. Thank you for your patronage.

Welcome to Elemental Rolls Streaming Information page. Incase you are not aware, Elemental Roll is a company made up of a ton of nerds who like to play Tabletop Role Playing Games, many of them actually using dice made by Elemental Roll. And Because of that, we want to help share our nerdiness with the world, in more ways than just sharing dice. On Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays, Workers of Elemental Roll volunteer their time willingly to put on a show. Every four month we change game systems, from Dungeons and Dragons Fifth Edition to Vampire the Masquerade, to Pathfinder, to Call of Cthulhu. Our game is ran by a great group of people. We have our Game Master, otherwise known as GM, Sam Andersen, CEO, who has been playing tabletop roleplaying games for more than half a decade, and our players, including Isaac Johnsrud, Liam Henretty, Bren Stone, Sara Jane Smith, and Argus Ito. All of us come together to share the joy that we find in these games with the rest of the world, as well as model how to play games that may be less popular than others, but by no means less enjoyable to play with your friends. Come on down to join in the fun.
